Hello,
My name is John Guardado, I am a graduate student in Biochemistry and Molecular Biophysics (BMB) @ Caltech. My research sits at the intersection of structural biology, biophysics, and computation — I'm drawn to questions about how molecular structure gives rise to biological function.

Before graduate school, I graduated in 2023 from the Univeristy of San Francisco in Chemistry. Outside the lab, I enjoy playing rugby, reading, and mentoring.

I'm always happy to connect with fellow researchers, collaborators, or anyone curious about molecular biology.
